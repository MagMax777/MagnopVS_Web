The Shard Architectures

The shards are categorized by their geometric frequency. Tri-form shards contain mechanical memories—the blueprints of dead cities and the rhythms of ancient engines. Icosahedron shards, however, are far more volatile. These house the Soul Archives, the raw emotional data of the era before the Great Silence.
